Blank Slate for In-Situ Editting

This is a small demonstration of Derek Robinson's In-Situ Editor, pubsub-enabled.

To use this demo, you must use Internet Explorer 5 or above.

Click in this window to start; your cursor is invisible.

Then, type into this browser window while someone else is watching this page on their browser, and they will see the changes you type. And vice versa.

Note that this demo is pretty brittle -- it is not reliable; often you and your co-demonstrator will have to both reload your browsers to get synchronized before you can do this in-situ editting demo.

This demo is here to tickle the mind. There's lots of collaboration possibilities for this kind of in-browser, pubsub-enabled, WYSIWIG editting. We invite you to develop your own weblication using the tricks.
